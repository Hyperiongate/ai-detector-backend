How Speech Analysis Works

1. Audio Processing

We convert speech to text using advanced AI transcription, capturing every word with high accuracy.

2. Claim Extraction

Our AI identifies factual claims, statistics, and verifiable statements within the speech.

3. Fact Verification

Each claim is cross-referenced with trusted sources and fact-checking databases in real-time.

We calculate an overall truth score based on verified claims, speaker history, and source reliability.
