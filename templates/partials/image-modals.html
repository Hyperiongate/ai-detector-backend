Our Image Analysis Process

1. Image Upload & Preprocessing

Your image is securely uploaded and prepared for analysis. We extract metadata, normalize the image format, and prepare it for our AI models.

  • Secure HTTPS upload
  • Format validation
  • Resolution optimization
  • Metadata extraction

2. AI Detection Models

Multiple specialized AI models analyze your image for signs of artificial generation or manipulation.

  • GAN detection algorithms
  • Diffusion model signatures
  • Style transfer detection
  • Deepfake identification

3. Forensic Analysis

Deep pixel-level examination to detect editing, cloning, or other manipulations.

  • Error level analysis
  • Clone detection
  • Compression artifacts
  • Noise pattern analysis

4. Metadata Examination

Comprehensive analysis of embedded data to verify authenticity and origin.

  • EXIF data validation
  • Timestamp verification
  • Camera signature matching
  • Editing software traces

5. Confidence Scoring

All findings are combined using our proprietary algorithm to generate trust scores and confidence levels.

  • Weighted evidence scoring
  • Cross-validation checks
  • Uncertainty quantification
  • Final verdict calculation
