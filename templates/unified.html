How We Detect AI Content

Our AI detection system uses multiple advanced techniques to identify AI-generated content:

  • Pattern matching against known AI model outputs (GPT-4, Claude, Gemini)
  • Perplexity analysis: Measures how predictable the text is (AI text tends to be more predictable)
  • Burstiness analysis: Examines sentence length variation (humans write with more variation)
  • Vocabulary diversity: Analyzes word choice patterns and repetition
  • Stylistic fingerprinting: Identifies subtle patterns unique to AI models

Understanding Perplexity

Perplexity measures how "surprised" a language model is by the text. AI-generated content tends to be more predictable (low perplexity: 20-50), while human writing is more varied and unpredictable (higher perplexity: 50-200).
